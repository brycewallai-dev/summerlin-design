The approach

Designed for how you actually live, not how a room photographs.

Most design firms hand off your project to junior staff after the initial consult. That’s not how this works. You work directly with one designer — the same person who heard your brief on day one is the same person who signs off on final installation.

Our process

From first conversation to final walkthrough.

A clear process means no surprises. Here’s how a project moves from initial conversation to a finished home.

01

Complimentary Consultation

A 30-minute conversation to understand your home, your goals, and how you actually live in the space. You’ll leave with a clear sense of scope and next steps — no pressure, no obligation.

02

Proposal & Design Agreement

A written proposal with defined scope, fee structure, and a clear payment schedule before any design work begins. No surprises on scope or cost.

03

Concept & Space Planning

Floor plans, material palettes, and design concepts built around how your family actually uses the space — not just how it looks on a mood board.

04

Selections & Procurement

Every furniture, fixture, and finish item sourced, ordered, and tracked in-house. One point of contact. One timeline. No vendor chasing on your end.

05

Installation & Final Walkthrough

On-site for delivery and installation, with a final walkthrough to confirm every detail is right before the project is closed.
