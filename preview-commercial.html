The problem

Our process

A clear process. No surprises. No scope creep.

Corporate clients need to know what they’re getting and what it costs before work begins. Here’s exactly how a project moves from first conversation to final walkthrough.

01

Complimentary Space Consultation

A 30-minute conversation, in person or virtual, to understand your space, your goals, and your timeline. You’ll leave with a clear sense of scope and next steps — no pressure, no obligation.

02

Proposal & Fee Structure

A written proposal with defined scope, a clear fee structure, and a payment schedule before any design work begins. You’ll know your total investment upfront.

03

Concept & Space Planning

Space plans, material palettes, and design concepts built around how your business actually functions day to day — not just how a room looks in a photo.

04

Selections & Procurement

We source and manage every furniture, fixture, and material order — vendor coordination, lead times, and logistics included, so your team doesn’t have to chase a single delivery.

05

Installation & Final Walkthrough

On-site oversight through installation, with a final walkthrough to confirm every detail matches the approved plan before we call it done.
