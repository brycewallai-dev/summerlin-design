The problem

Most offices weren't designed. They just happened.

Furniture gets ordered piecemeal. A conference room becomes a storage room. The waiting area still has the same chairs from move-in day. None of it is anyone's fault — running a business doesn't leave time to think about space planning, finish selections, or furniture procurement.

But your space is doing more work than you think. It's the first thing a new client sees when they walk in, the first impression a job candidate forms before you say a word, and the daily environment your team spends 40+ hours a week inside.

We handle the entire process — design, sourcing, and installation — so your space matches the caliber of the business you're running.

Our process

A clear process. No surprises. No scope creep.

Corporate clients need to know what they're getting and what it costs before work begins. Here's exactly how a project moves from first conversation to final walkthrough.

01

Complimentary Space Consultation

A 30-minute conversation, in person or virtual, to understand your space, your goals, and your timeline. You'll leave with a clear sense of scope and next steps — no pressure, no obligation.

02

Proposal & Fee Structure

You'll receive a written proposal with defined scope, a clear fee structure, and a payment schedule before any design work begins. You'll know your total investment upfront.

03

Concept & Space Planning

Space plans, material palettes, and design concepts tailored to how your business actually functions day to day — not just how a room looks in a photo.

04

Selections & Procurement

We source and manage every furniture, fixture, and material order — vendor coordination, lead times, and logistics included, so your team doesn't have to chase a single delivery.

05

Installation & Final Walkthrough

On-site oversight through installation, with a final walkthrough to confirm every detail matches the approved plan before we call it done.
