About Summerlin Design

Ten years. 250 projects. One designer.

Interior design across Orange County, handled start to finish by the same person who takes the brief.

Leslie Wall

The work is straightforward. The judgment behind it took a decade to build.

250+ Completed Projects
10+ Years Experience
30mi Service Radius

Summerlin Design was built around a single idea: that clients deserve to work directly with the person doing the work. Not an account manager. Not a rotating team of junior staff. The same designer from first conversation to final walkthrough.

That designer is Leslie Wall. Based in Ladera Ranch, she has spent over a decade designing homes across Orange County — from single-room refreshes to full new-builds. 250+ completed projects. Every one managed from brief through installation without handoffs.

The residential work built a foundation that most commercial designers don’t have: an understanding of how a space actually lives, not just how it photographs. That eye now informs commercial projects — offices, medical practices, hospitality spaces — where the same discipline of selecting for function, not just form, is what separates a well-designed space from a well-staged one.

Summerlin Design serves Orange County within a 30-mile radius of Ladera Ranch, including Irvine, Newport Beach, Mission Viejo, Aliso Viejo, Coto de Caza, Rancho Santa Margarita, Lake Forest, Dana Point, San Clemente, and surrounding areas.

Licensed & Insured

Summerlin Design carries full professional liability and general liability insurance. All projects are covered under a formal design agreement before work begins.

The approach

Three things that don’t change across residential or commercial work.

Direct.

You work with the designer — not an account manager or project coordinator. Every call, every decision, every answer comes from the person doing the work.

Managed.

Every vendor, every order, every delivery is handled in-house. Furniture, fixtures, and materials sourced, coordinated, and installed without you chasing a single timeline.

Considered.

Ten years of residential precision applied to every space. Selections are made for how a room functions on a Tuesday morning, not just how it photographs.
