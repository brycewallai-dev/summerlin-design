Summerlin Design is the practice of Leslie , based in Ladera Ranch, California. The studio has spent the last ten years building one of Orange County's most experienced residential design practices — 250 completed projects and counting, across homes from Ladera Ranch to Newport Beach, Coto de Caza, and Laguna Niguel.

The work has always been hands-on. Every project — whether a single room or a full new-build — is managed directly, from initial concept through final installation. No junior designers, no account managers, no handoffs. The person you meet in the first conversation is the person who does the work.

In 2025, the studio began accepting commercial clients. The same approach that built a 250-project residential track record — direct access, a clear written process, and accountable procurement — applies directly to the offices, medical practices, and hospitality spaces across Orange County that deserve better than a generic corporate design firm.

If you're looking for a designer who will know your project as well as you do, from first conversation to final walkthrough, that's what this practice is built for.

How we work

The same principles, whether it’s a living room or a law office.

Direct access, always.

You work with the designer, not a liaison. Every decision, every question, every update goes through the same person who built your design — from the first conversation to the final walkthrough.

Process before execution.

Every project starts with a written scope, timeline, and fee structure. You know what you're getting and what it costs before anything begins. No surprises, no scope creep that you didn't approve first.

Designed to function, not just to photograph.

Good design works at 8am on a Tuesday, not just in a portfolio shot. 250 projects of residential work builds the kind of judgment that distinguishes materials that hold up from ones that look great in a showroom and don't survive a year of real use.
